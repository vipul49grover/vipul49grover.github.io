Failed in Class 9
		
I failed in Class 9. I was dumb as a school student. Did not understand most subjects. Everyone criticized me, until one day my brother appreciated me for my notes making ability while I was struggling with my holiday homework.
From that very moment, something changed in my life. I never guessed that life would take a U Turn from thereon. Yes, it just takes one appreciation to turn a life on its head.

Today, my writing ability has grown big enough that I am a bestselling author of multiple books including  From the Rat Race to Financial Freedom, Happiness Unlimited and Dream On !!

I learnt to appreciate everyone for anything that I find good in them. There is no better service we can do to the mankind. 

Have you genuinely appreciated anyone today? Go on, find a good quality around you and appreciate. It does not matter even if the quality is too small, what matters is how genuinely you appreciate.
It can just elevate someone's day or even his or her entire life.
